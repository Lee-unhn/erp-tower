模組 0：共通基礎Module 0: Foundation

所有 Financials 模組共享的地基——企業結構、科目表、分類帳與功能設定管理 The bedrock every Financials module shares — enterprise structure, chart of accounts, ledger, and Functional Setup Manager

Enterprise Structure Legal Entity Ledger (4C) Chart of Accounts SLA

為什麼共通基礎很重要？Why does the Foundation matter?

虛構食品集團「匯智食品（GlobalTaste）」剛買下 Oracle Fusion Cloud Financials，準備上線。財務長急著開發票、跑總帳，但顧問告訴他：在你打第一張憑證之前，得先把「企業結構」搭好——這就像蓋大樓要先打地基。地基沒對，之後每張分錄都會歪。 Fictional food group "GlobalTaste" has just purchased Oracle Fusion Cloud Financials and wants to go live. The CFO is eager to enter invoices and run the ledger, but the consultant explains: before the first journal is posted, the enterprise structure must be built — like laying a foundation before erecting a tower. Get the foundation wrong and every later entry tilts.

匯智食品的地基包含三層：法人（Legal Entity）代表「誰簽約、誰欠錢、誰報稅」；分類帳（Ledger）決定「用哪套科目表、哪個會計曆、哪種幣別、哪種會計方法」；業務單位（Business Unit）則是應付、應收等子帳交易實際發生的地方。這三者透過會計科目表（Chart of Accounts）的「平衡段值」串在一起，缺一不可。 GlobalTaste's foundation has three layers: the Legal Entity says "who signs, who owes, who files tax"; the Ledger decides "which chart of accounts, which calendar, which currency, which accounting method"; the Business Unit is where subledger transactions such as Payables and Receivables actually occur. All three are tied together through the balancing segment value in the Chart of Accounts — none can be skipped.

🔑
核心場景：Oracle Fusion 的所有 Financials 模組（AP、AR、FA、GL、Cash、Tax）都建立在同一套共通企業結構之上。這些設定大多在使用初期就要做對，因為科目表、平衡段、是否使用多平衡段等選擇「一旦開始交易就無法更改」。這就是為什麼共通基礎是所有 ERP 課程的必修前置。 Core scenario: Every Fusion Financials module (AP, AR, FA, GL, Cash, Tax) sits on the same shared enterprise structure. Most of these settings must be right up front, because choices like the chart of accounts, balancing segments, and whether to use multiple balancing segments cannot be changed once you begin transacting. That is why the Foundation is a prerequisite for all ERP courses.

深入解析：企業結構（Enterprise Structure）Deep Dive: Enterprise Structure

▼

企業結構就是你的公司結構。企業（Enterprise）是集團；集團底下是法人（Legal Entity），法人以科目表中的平衡段值表示；法人在業務單位（Business Unit）中發生交易；交易透過子帳結算後轉入主分類帳（Primary Ledger），必要時再向上合併到公司合併分類帳。 The enterprise structure is your company structure. The Enterprise is the group; beneath it are Legal Entities, each represented by a balancing segment value in the CoA; legal entities incur transactions within Business Units; those transactions settle in subledgers and transfer up into a Primary Ledger, then optionally consolidate into a corporate ledger.

深入解析：法人（Legal Entity）與法律登記Deep Dive: Legal Entity & Legal Registration

▼

法人是法律賦予權利與義務的主體：有權擁有財產、交易，有義務清償債務、向稅務機關與主管機關報告。法人是任何交易的簽約方，擁有銀行現金、承擔付供應商的義務、繳稅、進行公司間交易。 A legal entity is a recognized party given rights and responsibilities by legislation: the right to own property and trade, the responsibility to repay debt and account to tax authorities and regulators. The legal entity is the contracting party on any transaction — it owns the cash in the bank, bears the obligation to pay suppliers, pays taxes, and performs intercompany trading.

深入解析：業務單位（Business Unit）Deep Dive: Business Unit

▼

業務單位被多數子帳（AP、AR）用來分割資料。建立時指派一個「預設法人」，但它可以代表同一分類帳下的多個法人處理交易——這就是共享服務中心（Shared Service Center）模式：一個應付部門可代多個業務單位鍵入供應商發票。業務單位也承載「業務功能」，例如採購業務單位。 Business units are used by most subledgers (AP, AR) to partition data. Each is assigned a default legal entity, but it can process transactions for many legal entities within the same ledger — this is the Shared Service Center model: one payables department entering supplier invoices for many business units. A business unit also carries business functions, e.g. a procurement business unit.

業務單位如何分割資料（匯智食品）How a Business Unit partitions data (GlobalTaste)

匯智食品把外銷與內銷分成兩個業務單位。進到外銷業務單位的應付／應收時，只看得到外銷的發票；進到內銷則只看到內銷的發票。資料因此乾淨隔離。 GlobalTaste splits export and domestic sales into two business units. Inside the export business unit's Payables/Receivables you see only export invoices; inside domestic you see only domestic invoices. Data is thus cleanly isolated.

💡
熟悉舊版 EBS 的人：業務單位類似「營運單位（Operating Unit）」，但功能更多——可指派業務功能、支援共享服務、跨法人處理。 If you know legacy EBS: a business unit is similar to an "operating unit" but with more functionality — assignable business functions, shared-service support, and cross-legal-entity processing.

深入解析：分類帳（Ledger）與 4CDeep Dive: Ledger & the 4C

▼

分類帳由「4C」定義：Chart of Accounts（科目表）、Calendar（會計曆）、Currency（幣別）、Convention（會計慣例，即會計方法 Accounting Method）。建立主分類帳（Primary Ledger）時，這四者是必填欄位。總帳在分類帳層運作，子帳則在業務單位層運作。 A ledger is defined by the "4C": Chart of Accounts, Calendar, Currency, and Convention (the accounting convention, i.e. the Accounting Method). When you create a Primary Ledger these four are mandatory fields. GL operates at the ledger level; subledgers operate at the business-unit level.

深入解析：科目表（Chart of Accounts）結構Deep Dive: Chart of Accounts Structure

▼

科目表在 Oracle 中是一種「會計彈性欄位（Accounting Flexfield）」。它由段（Segment）組成，每段掛一個值集（Value Set），值集是「值的容器」，控制長度、驗證型態等（GL 一律用 Independent 驗證）。段還有段標籤（Segment Label）賦予特殊功能。 In Oracle the chart of accounts is an "Accounting Flexfield." It is made of Segments, each attached to a Value Set — a "container for values" that controls length, validation type, etc. (GL always uses Independent validation). Segments also carry Segment Labels that grant special functions.

結構 vs. 實例（Structure vs. Instance）Structure vs. Instance

科目表結構（Structure）定義段的數量、順序、名稱、預設值集與段標籤。科目表實例（Instance）繼承結構的所有屬性（同段同順序），但可覆寫預設值集、指派唯一階層。最佳實務是一個結構只對一個實例。建好後必須「部署（Deploy）」——在 UI 看到綠色勾才可使用。 The CoA Structure defines the number, order, names, default value sets, and segment labels of the segments. The CoA Instance inherits all attributes of the structure (same segments, same order) but can override default value sets and assign unique hierarchies. Best practice is one structure to exactly one instance. After building it you must Deploy it — a green tick in the UI means it is usable.

深入解析：子帳會計（SLA）Deep Dive: Subledger Accounting (SLA)

▼

子帳會計（Subledger Accounting，SLA）是連接子帳（AP、AR、FA、Expenses…）與總帳的會計引擎。子帳交易（如發票驗證）發生後，SLA 依分類帳綁定的「會計方法（Accounting Method）」把交易翻譯成借貸分錄，再轉入總帳。會計方法由 Journal Line Rule（分錄行規則）、Account Rule（帳戶規則）等組成的 Journal Entry Rule Set 驅動。 Subledger Accounting (SLA) is the accounting engine that connects subledgers (AP, AR, FA, Expenses…) to General Ledger. After a subledger transaction (e.g. invoice validation), SLA translates it into debit/credit journals per the "Accounting Method" bound to the ledger, then transfers them to GL. The accounting method is driven by a Journal Entry Rule Set composed of Journal Line Rules and Account Rules.

SLA 為何重要：一份交易，多套帳Why SLA matters: one transaction, multiple books

同一筆子帳交易，SLA 可依不同會計方法產生給主分類帳（如美國 GAAP）與次分類帳（如 IFRS）各自的分錄——這就是「subledger 層」轉換所發生的地方。這讓一次交易就能同時滿足多個報表準則，不必重複鍵入。 For the same subledger transaction, SLA can produce separate journals for the primary ledger (e.g. US GAAP) and a secondary ledger (e.g. IFRS) per different accounting methods — this is where "subledger-level" conversion happens. It lets one transaction satisfy multiple reporting standards without re-keying.

常見問題FAQ

法人、業務單位、分類帳到底怎麼區分？How do I tell legal entity, business unit, and ledger apart?▼

法人＝法律上簽約報稅的主體（對應平衡段值）；業務單位＝子帳交易發生與分割的地方（AP/AR 資料按它切）；分類帳＝一組 4C 的記帳帳，總帳在此運作。一句話：法人管法律、業務單位管交易、分類帳管會計。Legal entity = the legally contracting, tax-filing party (maps to a balancing segment value); business unit = where subledger transactions occur and are partitioned (AP/AR data splits by it); ledger = a book with one set of 4C where GL runs. In short: legal entity owns the law, business unit owns the transaction, ledger owns the accounting.

一個業務單位可以跨多個法人嗎？Can one business unit span multiple legal entities?▼

可以，只要那些法人都屬於該業務單位所指向的同一分類帳。建立時指派一個「預設法人」，但實際交易可代多個法人處理——這就是共享服務中心模式。Yes, as long as those legal entities belong to the same ledger the business unit points to. It is assigned one default legal entity but can process transactions for many — the shared service center model.

為什麼有些設定「開始交易後就不能改」？Why are some settings "unchangeable once you transact"?▼

科目表結構、是否使用多平衡段、成本中心段的有無、會計曆首期一旦啟用就鎖定，因為既有交易與餘額已依它們記錄，事後更改會破壞歷史一致性。所以規劃階段要一次想清楚。The CoA structure, whether you use multiple balancing segments, the presence of a cost center segment, and the calendar's first opened period lock once used, because existing transactions and balances are already recorded against them and changing them would break historical consistency. So decide fully during planning.

Rapid Implementation 能完成整個導入嗎？Can Rapid Implementation complete the whole rollout?▼

不能。Rapid Implementation 任務清單只涵蓋約 90 個關鍵任務，讓你快速上線日常使用；仍需用標準財務任務清單（900+ 任務）補充額外設定資料。No. The Rapid Implementation task list covers only about 90 critical tasks to get you live quickly; you still need the standard financial task list (900+ tasks) to add and update additional setup data.

值集（Value Set）可以共用嗎？Can value sets be shared?▼

可以跨科目表共用，也可在同一科目表內重用（如公司段與公司間段值相同時）。但要注意段值安全是「按值集」施加的——共用值集被加密時，兩個段會一起被限制。用公司間又要做安全時，建議分開值集。Yes, across charts of accounts and reused within one chart (e.g. when the company and intercompany segments share values). But segment value security is applied per value set — securing a shared value set restricts both segments. If you use intercompany and need security, set up separate value sets.

SLA 跟總帳分錄有什麼關係？How does SLA relate to GL journals?▼

子帳交易本身不是分錄；SLA 用分類帳的會計方法把它翻譯成借貸分錄（帶來源＝Payables、類別＝Purchase Invoice 等），才轉進總帳。同一交易可依不同會計方法同時餵給主帳與次帳，滿足多套準則。A subledger transaction is not itself a journal; SLA translates it into debits/credits (tagged Source = Payables, Category = Purchase Invoice, etc.) per the ledger's accounting method, then transfers it to GL. The same transaction can feed the primary and secondary ledgers under different methods to satisfy multiple standards.

供應商據點與據點指派Supplier Sites & Site Assignments

▾

供應商據點（Supplier Site）建立在全域供應商記錄之下，代表「某個業務單位」與「該供應商」之間的關係——把它想成一組定義雙方往來的規則與細節：付款條件、發票處理設定、地址、控制與合規政策。每個地址要先指定用途：Ordering（管理採購單）、Remit to（送付款）、RFQ/Bidding（僅報價議標）。地址最終用來建立供應商據點。一個據點可指派給多個業務單位——假設匯智食品有四個製造廠（各為一個業務單位）都向同一供應商採購，不必建四個據點，一個據點指派給四個業務單位即可，維持地址、控制與預設條件的一致性。 A Supplier Site sits under the global supplier record and represents the relationship between a specific Business Unit and that supplier — a set of rules and details governing their dealings: payment terms, invoice processing, addresses, controls, and compliance. Each address first gets a purpose: Ordering (manage POs), Remit to (send payments), RFQ/Bidding (quotations only). Addresses ultimately create supplier sites. One site can be assigned to multiple BUs — if GlobalTaste Foods runs four plants (each a BU) buying from the same supplier, you assign one site to all four rather than creating four sites, keeping addresses, controls, and defaults consistent.

據點指派（Site Assignment）是關鍵：每個供應商據點必須有一筆有效指派，才能對該據點建立交易（採購單或發票）。若指派不存在或失效，就無法交易。指派控制「哪個客戶業務單位可與此據點往來」，並定義「開帳業務單位」（Bill-to BU）——承擔採購負債的組織，多數情況客戶業務單位與開帳業務單位相同。若兩者不同，可能產生公司間會計。指派模型的核心精神：善用集中化採購組織議定條件與政策，讓所有被指派的客戶業務單位共用同一套據點條件，避免各自為政。 Site Assignment is critical: every site must have an active assignment to create transactions (POs or invoices) against it. 為什麼建供應商是採購的流程，不是應付的？Why is creating suppliers a Procurement process, not AP?▾
因為供應商主檔為採購與應付共用，且屬於採購應用擁有的資料。若公司同時導入採購與財務，建供應商由採購負責。若只導入財務，應付使用者必須被設為採購代理人才有權限建立與維護供應商。Because the supplier master is shared and owned by Procurement. 潛在供應商和可支出供應商差在哪？Prospective vs Spend Authorized — what's the difference?▾
潛在（Prospective）只能參與招標議價與資格審查，不能下單或開發票；可支出（Spend Authorized）才能完整往來。潛在升級為可支出需發起供應商請求，或該供應商贏得議價得標。Prospective can only join sourcing and qualification, not order or invoice; Spend Authorized can trade fully. Promotion requires a supplier request or winning an awarded negotiation.
為什麼沒有據點指派就不能開發票？Why can't I invoice without a site assignment?▾
因為據點指派控制「哪個業務單位可與此據點交易」並定義開帳業務單位。若指派不存在或失效，系統無法為該據點建立採購單或發票。這是新手建完供應商卻無法開發票的常見原因。Because the site assignment controls which BU can transact with the site and defines the Bill-to BU. 貸項通知和借項通知該用哪個？Credit Memo or Debit Memo — which do I use?▾
兩者金額都是負的、都沖減應付。若供應商主動開了貸項給你，登錄為貸項通知（Credit Memo）；若供應商沒給、你需自行記錄退貨或折讓，登錄為借項通知（Debit Memo）。Both are negative and reduce payables. If the supplier issues you a credit, record a Credit Memo; if they don't and you need to document the return/adjustment yourself, record a Debit Memo.

為什麼發票處理很重要？Why does Invoice Processing matter?

匯智食品（虛構公司）每月收到上千張供應商發票——有的對應採購單、有的沒有；金額對得上採購單、也有的多算幾塊錢。若把發票直接付出去，就等於放棄了「收多少、驗多少、付多少」的三重把關。發票處理鏈就是這道閘門：發票登入後，必須先「驗證」，通過才產生會計分錄與付款排程。 MatuWise Foods (a fictional company) receives thousands of supplier invoices a month — some matched to POs, some not; some agreeing with the PO price, some off by a few dollars. Paying an invoice without control means giving up the "ordered vs received vs billed" three-way check. Invoice processing is that gate: after entry, an invoice must be validated before accounting entries and a payment schedule are created.

舉例：採購單訂 10 台工業攪拌機、單價 $29.95，收貨部門只入庫 8 台。供應商卻寄來 10 台的發票。若沒有比對與保留機制，帳上就會多認 2 台的負債、多付 $59.90。Oracle Payables 的驗證程序會偵測「開單數量 > 收貨數量」，自動掛上收貨保留（Received Quantity Hold），擋住付款，直到收貨補齊或發票更正。 Example: a PO for 10 industrial mixers at $29.95, but Receiving only booked 8. The supplier still bills for 10. Without matching and holds, the ledger would over-recognize 2 units of liability and overpay $59.90. Payables validation detects "billed qty > received qty" and auto-applies a Received Quantity Hold, blocking payment until the receipt is corrected or the invoice adjusted.

🔑
核心場景：發票驗證是所有下游動作（分錄、核准、付款）的前置條件。未驗證的發票不能付、也不能過帳。變異超出容差就掛保留，保留不釋放就付不出去。 Core scenario: Validation is the prerequisite for every downstream action (accounting, approval, payment). An unvalidated invoice can't be paid or accounted. Variance beyond tolerance triggers a hold; an unreleased hold blocks payment.

容差設定與比對保留Tolerances & Matching Holds

Set by quantity or amount, each as a percent or fixed value. Key trap: 0% means "no variance allowed", while leaving it blank means "infinite tolerance" — so always enter a value or any variance passes. Tolerances must be assigned to supplier sites to take effect.

常見問題 FAQFAQ

驗證和核准有什麼不同？What's the difference between validation and approval?▾

驗證是系統性檢查（容差、分配、稅、期別、資金）並掛/釋保留，是付款與過帳的前置；核准是工作流層級的人工放行，依組織政策決定是否必要。兩者都通過才可付款。Validation is a systematic check (tolerances, distributions, tax, period, funds) that applies/releases holds and is the prerequisite for payment and accounting. Approval is a workflow-level human sign-off, required only if the org's policy demands it. Both must pass to pay.

容差留空和設 0% 差在哪？Blank tolerance vs 0% — what's the difference?▾

0% 表示「完全不允許變異」，任何差異都會掛保留；留空表示「無限容差」，等於放行所有差異。務必給值，別把留空當成嚴格。0% means "no variance allowed" — any difference triggers a hold. Blank means "infinite tolerance" — every difference passes. Always enter a value; don't mistake blank for strict.

為什麼三方比對的發票一驗證就掛保留？Why does a three-way invoice hold on validation?▾

三方比對要求發票、PO、收貨三者齊備。若尚未建立收貨，驗證會偵測「開單量 > 收貨量」而掛收貨數量保留。補做收貨後重新驗證即釋放。Three-way requires invoice, PO, and receipt. If no receipt exists yet, validation detects "billed qty > received qty" and applies a Received Quantity Hold. Create the receipt and re-validate to release it.

系統保留可以手動釋放嗎？Can system holds be released manually?▾

多數不行。系統保留（如收貨量、分配差異）須先解除例外條件——補收貨、做價格/數量更正、修正分配——再重新驗證才會釋放。手動保留才可直接於 Manage Holds 釋放。Mostly no. System holds (received qty, distribution variance) require resolving the exception first — receive goods, do a price/qty correction, fix distributions — then re-validate to release. Only manual holds can be released directly in Manage Holds.

拆分過的分期為什麼驗證後沒重算？Why isn't my split installment recalculated?▾

刻意如此。一旦手動編輯或拆分分期，驗證就不會再自動重算，以免蓋掉你的手動安排。若要重算，須是未手動改過的分期，且啟用 Recalculate Invoice Installments。By design. Once you manually edit or split an installment, validation will not recalculate it, so it won't overwrite your manual arrangement. Recalculation only applies to un-edited installments with Recalculate Invoice Installments enabled.

分配集適合什麼情境？When should I use a distribution set?▾

未比對到 PO 的費用發票（如租金、廣告）需分攤到多部門時。已知比例用含百分比分配集（如 30/45/25%）；比例每月變動（如依人頭數）則用不含百分比、驗證時手動填金額。For non-PO expense invoices (rent, advertising) that split across departments. Use a with-% set when the split is known (e.g. 30/45/25%); use a without-% set when it varies monthly (e.g. by headcount) and enter amounts at entry.

深入：預付與預付沖銷Deep Dive: Prepayment & Prepayment Application

▶

預付（Prepayment）是你在供應商尚未交貨或提供服務前先付的預付款。在 Oracle Payables 它是一種「發票型別」，日後可沖抵（apply）標準發票，把標準發票要付的金額扣掉先前已付的預付。 A prepayment is an advance you pay a supplier before goods or services are rendered. In Oracle Payables it is a type of invoice that can later be applied against a standard invoice, reducing that invoice's amount by what was prepaid.

⚠️
硬性規則：預付發票必須先「全額付清」，才能沖抵到標準發票。沒付清的預付不能拿來抵。 Hard rule: a prepayment invoice must be fully paid before it can be applied to a standard invoice. An unpaid prepayment cannot be used to offset anything.

預付的三個動作The three prepayment actions

  • 建立預付發票——記錄一筆預付款。Create a prepayment invoice — record the advance.
  • 把預付沖抵到標準發票（apply）——供應商正式開票後，把已付清的預付抵掉標準發票金額。Apply a prepayment to a standard invoice — once the real invoice arrives, offset it with the paid prepayment.
  • 從標準發票取消沖抵（unapply）——若抵錯了發票，用此動作退回重抵。Unapply a prepayment from the standard invoice — if applied to the wrong invoice, reverse and re-apply.

常見問題FAQ

單筆付款與付款批次（PPR）差在哪？Single payment vs. Payment Process Request?▶

單筆付款用於緊急、非週期、單張發票（或同一供應商據點的少數幾張），可無視到期日；PPR 是批次引擎，用選取條件一次撈大量到期發票、自動分組付款、產出付款檔。日常量大用 PPR，臨時急件用單筆。Single payment is for urgent, off-cycle, one-off invoices (or a few from the same supplier site), and can ignore due dates; the PPR is a batch engine that pulls many due invoices via selection criteria, groups payments, and generates files. Use PPR for volume, single payment for emergencies.

Quick、Manual、Refund 三種付款型別怎麼選？Quick vs. Manual vs. Refund payment types?▶

Quick=系統直接開一張新付款並產出付款檔；Manual=在系統外已手開支票，事後回系統補記；Refund=記錄供應商把錢退回來的收款。Quick = the system creates a fresh payment and produces the file; Manual = a check was hand-issued outside the system and is recorded after the fact; Refund = records money a supplier returns to you.

為什麼我的預付款不能沖抵標準發票？Why can't my prepayment be applied to the invoice?▶

最常見原因是預付發票尚未「全額付清」。Oracle 硬性要求：預付必須先付清，才能沖抵到標準發票。先完成預付付款，再回來 apply。The most common cause: the prepayment invoice isn't fully paid. Oracle strictly requires a prepayment to be paid in full before it can be applied. Pay the prepayment first, then apply.

Pay Alone 發票和其他發票能合併付款嗎？Can a Pay Alone invoice be bundled with others?▶

不行。系統會在多選發票時自動排除 Pay Alone 發票；若先選了 Pay Alone 發票，就無法再加其他發票到同一張付款。它必須單獨開一張付款。No. The system automatically excludes Pay Alone invoices when selecting multiple; if you pick a Pay Alone invoice first, no others can be added to that payment. It must be paid on its own.

2/10 net 30 到底省多少？值得早付嗎？How much does 2/10 net 30 save?▶

10 天內付享 2% 折扣，NT$1,000 只付 NT$980，省 NT$20。換算年化報酬極高（形同放棄 20 天資金換 2%），現金充裕時通常值得早付；系統會依付款條件自動算出折扣日與金額。Pay within 10 days for 2% off — NT$1,000 becomes NT$980, saving NT$20. Annualized this is a very high return (forgoing ~20 days of cash for 2%), so early payment usually pays off when cash allows; the system computes the discount date and amount from the term.

同一張發票到期日為什麼跟同事算的不一樣？Why does my due date differ from a colleague's?▶

因為「條件日期基準」不同。到期日 = 基準日 + 條件天數；基準可設發票日、系統日或收貨日。同樣 net 30，若一人用發票日、一人用收貨日，到期日就會差。檢查 Manage Invoice Options 的設定。Because the terms date basis differs. Due date = basis date + term days; the basis can be invoice date, system date or goods-received date. Same net 30 with different bases yields different due dates. Check Manage Invoice Options.

付款分錄裡的「現金清算」科目是什麼？What is the "Cash Clearing" account in payment entries?▶

當啟用「兌現時入帳」，付款當下先貸現金清算（過渡科目），等銀行實際兌現、對帳後才把清算轉為現金，用來承接付款送出到銀行扣款之間的時間差。未啟用時付款直接貸現金。When "account at payment clearing" is on, the payment first credits Cash Clearing (a transit account); only after the bank clears and reconciliation runs does clearing move to Cash — capturing the timing gap between issue and clearing. Otherwise the payment credits Cash directly.

為什麼費用報支很重要？Why do Expenses matter?

虛構公司「匯智食品」派區域經理 Jane 出差東京談通路。她先向公司申請一筆預借現金付飯店訂金，出差時餐費刷公司卡、計程車自掏腰包。回國後她要把這些散落的花費，變成一份能被主管核准、被稽核員檢查、最後由公司匯款給她（外加匯給發卡行）的「費用報支單」。這整條鏈就是 Oracle Fusion Expenses 要管的事。 Fictional company GlobalFood sends regional manager Jane to Tokyo to negotiate distribution. She first requests a cash advance to pay a hotel deposit; on the trip she puts meals on the corporate card and pays taxis out of pocket. Back home she must turn these scattered costs into one Expense Report that a manager can approve, an auditor can review, and that GlobalFood ultimately reimburses to her (and pays to the card issuer). That whole chain is what Oracle Fusion Expenses governs.

Expenses 不只是「輸入收據」。它替公司訂出可報支的政策（每日餐費上限、需附收據金額）、偵測違規與詐欺、把公司卡帳單與員工申報自動配對，並在核准後把款項乾淨地轉進 Payables 開立付款請求。做對了，員工快速拿到錢、財務有稽核軌跡、發卡行準時收款。 Expenses is more than "entering receipts." It defines what is reimbursable (daily meal caps, receipt thresholds), detects violations and fraud, auto-matches corporate card statements against employee claims, and — once approved — hands the amounts cleanly to Payables as payment requests. Done right, employees get paid fast, Finance keeps an audit trail, and the card issuer is paid on time.

🔑
核心場景：一份費用報支單可同時含「現金墊付」與「公司卡」交易，且可套用一筆「預借現金」沖銷。付給誰、付多少，完全取決於公司卡的付款模式（個人付／公司付／雙方付）與預借沖銷後的餘額。 Core scenario: One Expense Report can carry both cash (out-of-pocket) and corporate-card lines, and can have a cash advance applied against it. Who gets paid and how much depends entirely on the corporate-card payment option (Individual / Company / Both Pay) and the balance after the advance is netted off.

預借現金是出差前預付給員工、支應差旅雜支的款項。流程：員工提出申請 → 主管核准 → 稽核員核准並指定沖銷到期日（Due Date） → 執行「Process Expense Reimbursements and Cash Advances」產生付款請求 → 員工收款。回國後費用報支上的花費會把預借沖銷掉。 A cash advance is a pre-payment to an employee for travel and incidental costs. Flow: employee requests → manager approves → auditor approves and sets a Due Date by which it must be applied → run "Process Expense Reimbursements and Cash Advances" to create the payment request → employee is paid. Later the expense report nets the advance off.

沖銷有兩種模式（在 Expense System Options 設定）：自動——執行 Reimbursement 程序時把未沖銷的預借全數套到到期報支單上；手動——員工報支時自己套用，若不套用須填不套用原因。預借可用外幣（須開啟 Allow Reimbursement Currency）、可委派他人代填、單筆有最高金額上限（範例設 $1,000）。 Two application modes (set in Expense System Options): Automatic — the Reimbursement process applies all unapplied advances to due reports; Manual — the employee applies it during entry and must give a reason if not applying. Advances can be foreign-currency (needs Allow Reimbursement Currency), can be delegated, and have a per-advance cap (example: $1,000).

深入解析：稽核Deep Dive: Audit

▾

稽核員確保大家遵守差旅費用政策。哪些報支要抽核，由多種規則決定：稽核選取規則（Audit Selection Rules，如抽核某百分比、超過某金額、有政策違規者）、報支範本／類型規則、稽核清單規則（Audit List Rules，違規者自動入清單，清單成員一律抽核），以及收據與通知規則（哪種收據、何時視為缺件／逾期）。 Auditors ensure everyone follows T&E policy. What gets audited is driven by several rule types: Audit Selection Rules (a % of reports, above an amount, policy violators), Template/Type rules, Audit List Rules (violators auto-added; list members always audited), and Receipt & Notification Rules (which receipts, when they're missing/overdue).

預借現金一定要沖銷嗎？沒用完怎麼辦？Must a cash advance be applied? What about unused amounts?▾

稽核員核准時會指定「到期日」，員工須在該日前把預借套到費用報支上，否則成為逾期預借。沖銷後任何未用餘額會套到下一份報支，或由員工退還公司。The auditor sets a due date by which the employee must apply the advance to a report, or it becomes overdue. After applying, any unused amount rolls to the next report or is refunded by the employee to the company.

員工把私人消費刷到公司卡，錢怎麼收回？How is a personal charge on the corporate card recovered?▾

若申報為個人費用：在「公司付」模式下，個人卡費會從應付員工的現金費用中扣抵（範例中員工現金 $500 扣 $100 個人 = 實領 $400）；在「雙方付」模式下，員工自行付該筆給發卡行。If reported as personal: under Company Pay the personal card amount is offset against the employee's cash expenses (in the example, $500 cash − $100 personal = $400 paid); under Both Pay the employee pays that amount to the issuer directly.

發票一定要先做帳務才能付款嗎？Must the invoice be accounted before it can be paid?▾

不用。Reimbursement 程序在 Payables 產生的付款請求，只需驗證（validate）即可付款；建立會計分錄可以在付款之後進行。No. The payment request the Reimbursement process creates in Payables only needs to be validated to be paid; accounting can be created afterwards.

員工需要在 Payables 建供應商檔嗎？Do employees need a supplier record in Payables?▾

不需要。員工在系統中是「當事人（Party）」，付款請求可直接付給員工，無須供應商紀錄。（約聘工作者 Contingent Worker 的報支則轉為發票。）No. Employees are parties in the system; payment requests can pay them without a supplier record. (Contingent worker reports, however, become invoices.)

報支被 Payables 退回最常見的原因？Most common reasons a report is rejected by Payables?▾

會計日落在關帳期間（程序會推到下一個開放期間，故至少要有一個開放期間）、無效的分配科目、重複發票號、無效幣別代碼、缺匯率或付款方式。修正後可重跑程序。Accounting date in a closed period (the process advances to the next open period, so at least one must be open), invalid distribution account, duplicate invoice number, invalid currency code, missing exchange rate or payment method. Fix and rerun the process.

支出授權（Spend Authorization）和預借現金差在哪？Spend Authorization vs Cash Advance — what's the difference?▾

支出授權是對「未來估計花費」的事前核准（不付錢），可搭配預算控制檢核資金，核准後可能要求附在報支單上；預借現金則是實際先付一筆錢給員工，之後以報支沖銷。A spend authorization is pre-approval of estimated future spend (no money moves), optionally fund-checked via Budgetary Control and may need attaching to the report. A cash advance actually pays money up front, later netted off by the report.

為什麼應付關帳與會計很重要？Why does AP Accounting & Close matter?

常見問題 FAQFAQ

草稿（Draft）和正式（Final）差在哪？What's the difference between Draft and Final?▶

Draft 只在子帳建分錄，可在總帳前先預覽，適合測試改過的分錄規則集；分錄不會落 GL。Final 建正式分錄，才可轉入並過帳到總帳。改規則後最佳實務是先 Draft 驗證再 Final。Draft creates journals in the subledger only for preview before GL — ideal for testing modified rule sets; nothing reaches GL. Final creates postable journals eligible for transfer and posting. Best practice: Draft to validate, then Final.

Create Accounting 出現錯誤事件怎麼重跑？How do I reprocess errored events?▶

修正底層問題後，把「處理事件」參數選 Errors 只重跑先前失敗的事件。若失敗原因是無效科目，可用 Invalid Account 選項，系統會把無效科目換成該總帳定義的暫記（suspense）科目。要深入診斷可用 Error Events with Diagnostic。After fixing the root cause, set Process Events to Errors to reprocess only prior failures. For invalid-account failures, use Invalid Account — the system swaps to the ledger's suspense account. For deep troubleshooting, use Error Events with Diagnostic.

要依業務單位還是依總帳對帳？Reconcile by business unit or by ledger?▶

若組織用主平衡分段值代表業務單位，可依業務單位對帳，各單位自管。若非此對應，或採服務提供者集中付款模式，則必須依總帳對帳，且使用者需能存取該總帳下所有業務單位。If BUs are represented by primary balancing segment values, reconcile by BU with each unit self-managing. Otherwise, or under the centralized service-provider payment model, you must reconcile by ledger, and users need access to all BUs under that ledger.

我一定要用自動抵銷嗎？Must I use Automatic Offsets?▶

不一定。若很少開跨平衡分段的發票，可不開，讓 Create Accounting 用跨公司平衡規則補平。若常有跨平衡分段的費用／資產發票且希望負債分錄按分段拆開，就啟用自動抵銷。Not required. If you rarely enter invoices crossing balancing segments, leave it off and let Create Accounting balance via intercompany rules. If you frequently have cross-segment expense/asset invoices and want the liability split by segment, enable Automatic Offsets.

關帳前一定要跑 Create Accounting 嗎？Must Create Accounting run before close?▶

是。所有發票與付款都必須已產生會計並轉／過帳到總帳，該期間才沒有未過會計交易。有殘留就關不了帳，也會造成子帳與 GL 對不上。Yes. Every invoice and payment must be accounted and transferred/posted so no unaccounted transactions remain. Residuals block the close and cause subledger-to-GL mismatches.

為什麼改了規則集卻沒生效？Why didn't my rule set change take effect?▶

改完分錄規則集或會計方法後，必須在會計方法頁按「啟動（Activate）」重編引擎程式碼。所有指派的分錄規則集都要是 Active，Create Accounting 才會採用；未啟動的變更等於沒改。After changing a rule set or accounting method, you must Activate on the accounting method page to recompile the engine. All assigned rule sets must be Active for Create Accounting to use them; unactivated changes are ignored.

深入解析：交叉驗證規則Deep Dive: Cross-Validation Rules

▾

交叉驗證規則（Cross-Validation Rules，CVR）防止使用者組出你認定為無效的「整組」科目組合。每條規則由「條件過濾（Condition Filter）」與「驗證過濾（Validation Filter）」構成：條件成立時，強制另一段必須符合驗證條件。關鍵限制——CVR 只作用於「新」帳戶；若無效組合在建規則前已存在於科目組合表且啟用，仍可繼續使用，所以規則要在開始產生帳戶前就定義好。Cross-Validation Rules (CVR) prevent users from building whole combinations you deem invalid. Each rule has a Condition Filter and a Validation Filter: when the condition holds, another segment is forced to meet the validation. Key limit — CVRs apply to NEW accounts only; an invalid combination already in the code-combination table and enabled before the rule stays usable, so define rules before generating accounts.

深入解析：段值安全、控制帳戶與資料存取Deep Dive: Segment Value Security, Control Accounts & Data Access

▾

段值安全Segment Value Security

段值安全（Segment Value Security，SVS）限制使用者能存取「單一段的哪些值」（如只能看部門 110），涵蓋資料輸入、線上查詢與報表。運算子含 equals、between、is a descendant of、is last descendant of。有兩種版本：（1）傳統版——一啟用即「預設拒絕所有值」，必須手動建「所有值」規則並指派給使用者，否則使用者連值都看不到；（2）依業務功能版（by Business Function）——啟用後系統「自動產生所有值政策」給已驗證使用者角色，再針對需要限制者建規則，且可依 GL/AP/AR/資產等業務功能與唯讀/讀寫分別授權。兩者在企業層級只能擇一。Segment Value Security (SVS) restricts which values of a single segment a user can access (e.g. only dept 110), covering data entry, inquiry and reporting. Operators include equals, between, is a descendant of, is last descendant of. Two versions: (1) classic — enabling denies all values by default, so you must manually build an "all values" rule and assign it, or users see nothing; (2) by Business Function — enabling auto-generates an all-values policy to the authenticated user role, then you add restrictive rules, with grants per business function (GL/AP/AR/Assets) and read-only vs read-write. Only one version is allowed enterprise-wide.

SVS 規則是「累積」的：若一個角色給你成本中心 200、另一角色給你 300–500，兩者同時生效，你就有 200 及 300–500 的存取權。SVS 規則透過專屬的 SVS 角色掛給使用者。SVS rules are cumulative: if one role grants cost center 200 and another grants 300–500, both apply simultaneously — you get 200 and 300–500. SVS rules reach users through dedicated SVS roles.

控制帳戶Control Accounts

在自然帳戶段值的屬性上，可把某帳戶設為第三方控制帳戶，讓總帳「按第三方明細」維護餘額，並禁止手動日記帳過帳進去。典型是供應商負債、客戶應收——這些餘額應只來自 AP/AR 子帳，以利對帳。可選型別：客戶控制帳戶、供應商控制帳戶、第三方控制帳戶、限制 GL 手動日記帳、否（非控制帳戶）。改動此屬性後須執行「Inherit Segment Value Attributes」把變更擴散到現有科目組合。On the natural-account segment value attributes, an account can be set as a third-party control account so the GL maintains balances by third party and blocks manual journals from posting to it. Typical: supplier liability, customer receivables — those balances should come only from AP/AR subledgers, for clean reconciliation. Types: Customer, Supplier, Third-Party, Restrict GL Manual Journals, or No (not a control account). After changing this attribute, run Inherit Segment Value Attributes to propagate it to existing account combinations.

資料存取集Data Access Sets

資料存取集（Data Access Set）是 GL 安全的另一層：建立分類帳或分類帳集時會自動產生一個「完整分類帳」存取集。你也可自建，授予「整本分類帳」或「特定主要平衡段值」的存取，並分唯讀/讀寫。因平衡段值常代表法律個體，這常用來把使用者鎖到特定法律個體。關鍵：建了存取集還不夠，必須另外把它指派給「使用者 + 角色」的組合，使用者才真的看得到那本分類帳。A Data Access Set is another GL security layer: creating a ledger or ledger set auto-generates a full-ledger data access set. You can also build your own, granting full-ledger or specific primary-balancing-segment-value access, each read-only or read-write. Since balancing segment values often represent legal entities, this commonly locks users to specific legal entities. Key: creating the set is not enough — you must also assign it to a user + role combination before the user can actually see that ledger.

🔑
三道安全把關各司其職：交叉驗證規則管「哪些科目組合能建」、段值安全管「使用者能看/用哪些單一段值」、資料存取集管「使用者能存取哪本分類帳/哪個平衡段值」。三者疊加才是完整的 GL 存取控制。Three gates, distinct jobs: CVRs govern which combinations can be built, SVS governs which single segment values a user sees/uses, and Data Access Sets govern which ledger / balancing segment value a user can reach. Together they form the complete GL access model.

常見問題FAQ

次要分類帳與報表幣別差在哪？Secondary ledger vs reporting currency — what's the difference?▾

次要分類帳提供「不同的會計表述」，4C 任一都能與主要分類帳不同（含科目表、日曆、會計方法）。報表幣別「不是」不同表述，只是同一套表述換另一個幣別呈現——僅幣別與處理選項可不同，其餘必須一致。A secondary ledger provides a different accounting representation and can differ in any of the 4Cs (including COA, calendar, method). A reporting currency is NOT a different representation — it is the same representation in another currency; only currency and processing options may differ.

為什麼我建了交叉驗證規則，舊的無效組合還是能用？Why does an old invalid combination still work after I made a CVR?▾

交叉驗證規則只作用於「新」帳戶。若該無效組合在建規則前已存在於科目組合表且啟用，就不受規則約束。可用「Manage Cross-Validation Rule Violation」流程列出並選擇性停用違規組合（停用前需先把餘額轉出）。CVRs apply only to new accounts. If the invalid combination already existed and was enabled before the rule, it is exempt. Use the Manage Cross-Validation Rule Violation process to list and optionally disable violating combinations (move balances out first).

交叉驗證規則和段值安全有什麼不同？CVR vs segment value security?▾

交叉驗證規則管「整組科目組合的合法性」（哪些段值能組在一起）；段值安全管「單一段的存取權」（某使用者能不能看/用某個段值）。前者是資料完整性，後者是資料安全。CVRs govern the validity of whole combinations (which segment values may be combined); segment value security governs access to a single segment (whether a user may see/use a given value). One is data integrity, the other is data security.

啟用段值安全後使用者什麼都看不到，正常嗎？After enabling SVS users see nothing — is that normal?▾

傳統（非業務功能）版段值安全一啟用即「預設拒絕所有值」，你必須手動建一條「所有值」規則、指派給需要的使用者角色。依業務功能版則會自動產生所有值政策，不會發生全被鎖住的情況。Classic (non-business-function) SVS denies all values by default on enable — you must manually build an "all values" rule and assign it to the needed user roles. The by-Business-Function version auto-generates an all-values policy, so users are not locked out.

為什麼供應商負債帳戶不能手動做分錄？Why can't I manually journal into the supplier liability account?▾

因為它被設為「供應商控制帳戶」。此類帳戶餘額應只來自 AP 子帳，總帳禁止手動日記帳過帳，以免破壞 AP 對總帳的對帳。若要改此屬性，改完須執行 Inherit Segment Value Attributes 擴散到現有科目組合。Because it is a supplier control account. Such balances should come only from the AP subledger, so the GL blocks manual journals to protect AP-to-GL reconciliation. To change the attribute, run Inherit Segment Value Attributes afterward to propagate it to existing combinations.

為什麼有了 GL 角色還是進不了某本分類帳？I have a GL role but still can't enter a ledger — why?▾

GL 功能角色（如 General Accountant）只給功能，不給資料。要進特定分類帳，還需一個「資料存取集」並把它指派給「你的使用者 + 角色」。少了這一步，日記帳畫面就選不到那本分類帳。A GL functional role (e.g. General Accountant) grants function, not data. To enter a specific ledger you also need a data access set assigned to your user + role. Without that step the journals page won't list the ledger.

深入解析：過帳（條件、狀態、跨期連動）Deep Dive: Posting (Conditions, Statuses, Cross-Period Cascade)

▾

過帳（Posting）將傳票金額更新到總帳科目餘額，供財務報表與分析使用。過帳前傳票的完成狀態（Completion Status）必須為「完成 Complete」，且會計期間必須開啟。過帳時系統會再次驗證；成功後借貸金額寫入各科目。一旦過帳，傳票內容不可修改、不可刪除——只能複製或沖銷。Posting updates GL account balances so they feed financial reports and analysis. Before posting, a journal's Completion Status must be Complete and its period must be open. Posting re-validates the journal; on success, debit and credit amounts are written to each account. Once posted, a journal cannot be modified or deleted—only copied or reversed.

⚠️
跨期過帳的餘額連動：過帳到「較前期間」時，系統會自動更新所有後續期間的期初餘額（即使該期間已關閉）；過帳到「較前年度」時，損益科目影響會調整保留盈餘（Retained Earnings）餘額。建議只保留當期開啟、其餘期間關閉，以防錯期入帳。Cross-period cascade: Posting to a prior period auto-updates the opening balances of all later periods (even closed ones); posting to a prior year adjusts Retained Earnings for the effect on income/expense accounts. Best practice: keep only the current period open and close the rest to prevent wrong-period entries.

深入解析：自動過帳（AutoPost 準則集）Deep Dive: AutoPost (Criteria Sets)

▾

自動過帳（AutoPost）透過 AutoPost 準則集（Criteria Set）依條件（來源、類別、期間、餘額類型等）自動挑選並過帳批次，可排程定期執行。它對匯入傳票尤其重要——自動過帳能防止使用者編輯匯入資料，因為一旦竄改匯入資料，子帳與總帳之間就會出現永久性的不平衡（out-of-balance）。AutoPost uses AutoPost Criteria Sets to select and post batches automatically by conditions (source, category, period, balance type, etc.), and can be scheduled to run periodically. It matters most for imported journals—AutoPost prevents users from editing import data, because editing imported data creates a permanent out-of-balance between subledgers and GL.

💡
執行 Create Accounting 時可一次選擇「傳輸至 GL」與「在 GL 過帳」；若當下選「否」，之後就要排程 AutoPost 在 Journal Import 完成後執行，以自動完成過帳。試算表傳票（ADFDI）與匯入並過帳（Import and Post）選項也可直接觸發過帳。On Create Accounting you can choose "Transfer to GL" and "Post in GL" in one step; if you choose No, schedule AutoPost to run after Journal Import to complete posting. Spreadsheet (ADFDI) journals and the Import-and-Post option can also trigger posting directly.

深入解析：沖銷（資格、方法、自動沖銷準則）Deep Dive: Reversal (Eligibility, Method, Criteria Sets)

▾

沖銷（Journal Reversal）用於沖回應計、估計、暫時性調整、重分類，或單純修正錯誤。三種做法：(1) 手動選定並在當期產生沖銷傳票；(2) 手動選定並指定較後期間產生；(3) 用沖銷準則集（Reversal Criteria Sets）自動沖銷。Journal Reversal reverses accruals, estimates, temporary adjustments, reclassifications, or simply corrects errors. Three ways: (1) manually select and generate in the current period; (2) manually select and generate in a later period; (3) use Reversal Criteria Sets to reverse automatically.

自動沖銷準則與資格Auto-Reversal Criteria & Eligibility

沖銷準則集為每個傳票類別指定沖銷期間（Next period、Next non-adjusting period…）與方法，並指派給分類帳（可多帳共用）。可設「開啟期間後自動沖銷（Run AutoReverse After Open Period）」；但若開新期間後仍有人在前期補應計，那些不會被自動挑到，故常再另排程或手動執行。A Reversal Criteria Set assigns a reversal period (Next period, Next non-adjusting period…) and method per journal category, then is attached to a ledger (shareable across ledgers). "Run AutoReverse After Open Period" can be enabled; but accruals keyed in the prior period after the new one opens are not picked up, so teams often also schedule or run it manually.

自動沖銷資格：餘額類型須為實際（Actual）；類別須啟用自動沖銷；沖銷期間須開啟或可未來輸入；傳票須已過帳但尚未被沖銷；本身不可是沖銷傳票；不可是由來源傳票複製的報表幣別傳票（它們隨來源傳票一同被沖）；不可是來自凍結來源的子帳傳票。且任一傳票只能被沖銷一次——即使原沖銷傳票被刪除且從未過帳，此規則仍適用。Auto-reversal eligibility: balance type must be Actual; the category must be enabled for reversal; the reversal period must be open or future-enterable; the journal must be posted but not yet reversed; it cannot itself be a reversal journal; it cannot be a reporting-currency journal replicated from a source (those reverse with the source); and it cannot be a subledger journal from a frozen source. A journal can be reversed only once—even if the original reversal was deleted and never posted.

深入解析：暫記帳戶（借貸不平時的暫掛）Deep Dive: Suspense Account (Holding Out-of-Balance)

▾

暫記帳戶（Suspense Account）在傳票借貸不平時，把差額暫掛到指定科目，讓傳票能平衡並過帳、不卡在介面表。可設一個涵蓋所有來源與類別的通用暫記帳戶（來源與類別皆為 Other，即預設暫記帳戶），也可為特定「來源 + 類別」組合各設一個。匯入時只要來源啟用暫記且勾選「Post errors to suspense」，遇到無效科目組合等錯誤即掛入暫記。A Suspense Account parks the difference of an out-of-balance journal into a designated account so it can balance, post, and not get stuck in the interface table. You can set one generic suspense account covering all sources and categories (Source = Other, Category = Other, the default), or a specific one per Source + Category combination. On import, if the source enables suspense and "Post errors to suspense" is set, errors like invalid account combinations are parked into suspense.

常見問題 FAQFrequently Asked Questions

傳票過帳後還能改嗎？Can I edit a journal after it's posted?▾

不行。過帳後傳票不可修改也不可刪除，只能「複製」或「沖銷」。且任一傳票只能被沖銷一次。過帳前則幾乎什麼都能改。No. After posting, a journal cannot be modified or deleted—only copied or reversed, and it can be reversed only once. Before posting, almost anything can be changed.

「換借貸」和「變號」沖銷有何差別？What's the difference between Switch Dr/Cr and Change Sign?▾

換借貸把借方金額移到貸方欄、貸方移到借方欄；變號則保持在原欄位但金額變負數（以括號表示）。兩者淨效果相同，選擇取決於你的會計規範。Switch Dr/Cr moves debit amounts to the credit column and vice versa; Change Sign keeps amounts in the same column but negates them (shown in brackets). The net effect is the same—choice depends on your accounting rules.

為什麼匯入的子帳傳票絕不能用刪除來修正？Why must I never delete imported subledger journals to fix them?▾

子帳（如 AP、AR）傳輸至 GL 時會被標記為「已轉出」。若刪除這些匯入資料，子帳與總帳將出現永久不平衡且極難還原。正確做法是用匯入更正試算表線上更正後重新匯入，而非刪除。非 Oracle 來源的資料才可刪除、於來源系統更正後重載。Subledger data (AP, AR) is flagged as "transferred" when moved to GL. Deleting it creates a permanent out-of-balance that is very hard to restore. Correct it online via the import correction spreadsheet and re-import—do not delete. Only non-Oracle source data may be deleted, corrected in the feeder system, and reloaded.

暫記帳戶會自己把帳「做對」嗎？Does the suspense account "fix" my accounting?▾

不會。暫記帳戶只是把不平衡的差額暫掛，讓傳票能過帳、不卡住流程。餘額掛在暫記代表帳仍待釐清，月結前必須查明並把暫記帳戶清空。No. Suspense only parks the imbalance so the journal can post and not block the flow. A balance in suspense means the entry still needs investigation—clear it before close.

統計傳票為什麼不用借貸平衡？Why don't statistical journals need to balance?▾

統計傳票記錄的是非金額單位（如坪數、人數），不是金錢，因此可為單邊分錄、無須借貸相等。它們常作為分攤（Allocation）的計算基礎，也可與金額混用（若分類帳允許）。Statistical journals record non-monetary units (floor area, headcount), not money, so they can be one-sided and need not balance. They often serve as the basis for Allocations and can be mixed with monetary amounts if the ledger allows.

跨期過帳時，前期的傳票會影響現在的餘額嗎？Does back-dated posting affect current balances?▾

會。過帳到較前期間時，系統自動更新所有後續期間的期初餘額（即使已關閉）；過帳到較前年度時，損益影響會調整保留盈餘。可啟用「Notify When Prior Period Journal」提醒，避免誤入前期。Yes. Posting to a prior period auto-updates opening balances of all later periods (even closed ones); posting to a prior year adjusts Retained Earnings for income/expense effects. Enable "Notify When Prior Period Journal" to warn against wrong-period entry.

為什麼期間關帳與外幣很重要？Why do Period Close & Currency matter?

虛構公司「匯智食品」（HuiZhi Foods）以美金 USD 為功能貨幣，在英國有 GBP 子公司、在墨西哥有 MXN 子公司。每個月底，會計團隊都要把子帳（AP/AR/資產/成本）的交易全部過帳到總帳，跑分攤把總部成本攤到各部門，再把外幣餘額依期末匯率重估、把子公司餘額換算成報表幣別，最後才能關帳出報表。The fictional company "HuiZhi Foods" uses USD as its functional currency, with a GBP subsidiary in the UK and an MXN subsidiary in Mexico. Every month-end, accounting must post all subledger transactions (AP/AR/Assets/Cost) to GL, run allocations to spread corporate cost across departments, revalue foreign balances at period-end rates, translate subsidiary balances to the reporting currency, then close the period and publish reports.

若順序錯了——例如還沒重估就換算、或子帳期間還開著就關 GL——報表數字就不準。Oracle Fusion 用「期間生命週期 + 關帳監控器（Close Monitor）+ 嚴格關帳防呆」把這條收尾鏈管起來，讓月結可稽核、可重複。Get the order wrong—translate before revaluing, or close GL while subledger periods are still open—and the numbers are unreliable. Oracle Fusion governs this chain with a period lifecycle, a Close Monitor, and strict-close controls so month-end is auditable and repeatable.

核心場景：月結收尾的黃金順序是「過帳 → 分攤 → 重估 → 換算 → 關帳」。重估產生未實現損益（影響損益表），換算產生 CTA（影響權益）——兩者都源自外幣，但落點不同。Core scenario: the golden month-end order is Post → Allocate → Revalue → Translate → Close. Revaluation creates unrealized gain/loss (hits P&L); translation creates CTA (hits equity)—both stem from foreign currency but land in different places.

深入解析：外幣重估（Revaluation）Deep Dive: Revaluation

▶

重估是把外幣計價的餘額，依「期末匯率」重新調整其入帳（功能貨幣）價值，算出未實現損益。設定「重估定義」指定要重估的帳戶（多半是資產負債表帳戶）、匯率類型（Period End）、未實現損益科目與延展匯率天數。重估產生的分錄通常隔月自動迴轉，若外幣餘額仍未清就再重估一次，直到付清為止。Revaluation re-adjusts the accounted (functional-currency) value of foreign-denominated balances at the period-end rate to compute unrealized gain/loss. A Revaluation Definition names the accounts (usually balance-sheet), the rate type (Period End), the unrealized gain/loss accounts, and days-to-roll-forward. The resulting journal auto-reverses next period; if the balance is still open it's revalued again until paid.

情境：匯智食品的歐元應收帳款重估Scenario: Revaluing a EUR receivable at HuiZhi Foods

深入解析：換算（Translation）與重衡量Deep Dive: Translation & Remeasurement

▶

換算是把整個帳套的功能貨幣餘額，重述成另一個報表幣別，供線上查詢、報表與合併。跑換算需要「餘額層級報表幣別（balance-level reporting currency）」——沒有它就選不到目標幣別。順序上，換算要在所有分錄與重估都完成後才跑；之後若補分錄或改匯率，必須重跑換算。Translation restates an entire ledger's functional-currency balances into another reporting currency for inquiry, reporting, and consolidation. It requires a balance-level reporting currency—without one, no target currency can be picked. Run it after all journals and revaluation are done; if you post more or change rates, retranslate.

別搞混三個詞：換算（Translation）把功能貨幣→報表幣別，差額入權益（CTA）；重衡量（Remeasurement）把帳載貨幣→功能貨幣，把收入費用當作原本就以功能貨幣入帳，差額入淨利；轉換（Conversion）則是交易入帳當下即時把外幣換成功能貨幣。Don't confuse three terms: Translation functional→reporting, difference to equity (CTA); Remeasurement accounting→functional, treating revenue/expense as if booked in functional currency, difference to net income; Conversion the on-the-spot FX at transaction entry.

深入解析：CTA 累積換算調整與歷史匯率Deep Dive: CTA & Historical Rates

▶

CTA（Cumulative Translation Adjustment，累積換算調整）是換算的「配平帳」。因為資產負債用期末匯率、權益/資本用歷史匯率——不同匯率必然使換算後借貸不平，這個差就記進 CTA（權益）。CTA 也支援多重平衡分段。CTA (Cumulative Translation Adjustment) is translation's balancing account. Because assets/liabilities use the period-end rate while equity uses historical rates, the translated debits and credits won't balance—that gap goes to CTA (equity). CTA supports multiple balancing segments.

常見問題 FAQFAQ

重估和換算差在哪？What's the difference between revaluation and translation?▶

重估是把外幣未清餘額依期末匯率調整功能貨幣入帳值，算未實現損益（進損益表）；換算是把整個帳套的功能貨幣餘額重述成報表幣別供合併，差額進 CTA（權益）。順序上先重估、後換算。Revaluation adjusts open foreign balances at the period-end rate to compute unrealized gain/loss (P&L); translation restates the whole ledger's functional-currency balances into a reporting currency for consolidation, with the gap to CTA (equity). Revalue first, then translate.

為什麼跑換算時選不到目標幣別？Why can't I pick a target currency when translating?▶

因為該帳套沒有設「餘額層級報表幣別（balance-level reporting currency）」。換算需要它才能建立目標幣別的餘額；有設的帳套會自動帶入目標幣別。Because the ledger has no balance-level reporting currency. Translation needs one to build the target-currency balances; ledgers that have one default the target currency automatically.

關閉的期間可以再打開嗎？Can a closed period be reopened?▶

「關閉（Closed）」可以重新開啟補分錄；但「永久關閉（Permanently Closed）」不行，通常用於歸檔/清除前。啟用嚴格關帳有助於一次關對、不必回頭重開。A Closed period can be reopened for late entries; a Permanently Closed one cannot—it's used before archive/purge. Strict close helps you close correctly the first time and avoid reopening.

為什麼我產生分攤時清單裡看不到規則？Why is my allocation rule missing from the generate list?▶

多半是規則沒有成功部署（Deploy）。在 Calculation Manager 裡規則必須先 Validate 再 Deploy，才會出現在 Journals 頁的產生清單中。Usually it wasn't deployed successfully. In Calculation Manager a rule must be Validated then Deployed before it appears in the generate list on the Journals page.

重估分錄之後會怎樣？What happens to the revaluation journal afterward?▶

若類別設定為自動迴轉，重估分錄會在次月自動迴轉；若外幣餘額仍未清就再重估一次，如此循環直到收付為止——收付當下才會由子帳產生已實現損益。If the category is set to auto-reverse, the revaluation journal reverses next period; if the foreign balance is still open it's revalued again, repeating until settlement—only then does the subledger post a realized gain/loss.

重衡量（Remeasurement）和換算又不一樣？How is remeasurement different from translation?▶

重衡量把帳載貨幣的收入費用轉成功能貨幣，好像原本就以功能貨幣入帳，差額進淨利（有損益影響）；換算把功能貨幣轉報表幣別，差額進權益（CTA）。功能貨幣=母國貨幣但海外營運→重衡量；功能貨幣≠母國貨幣、海外投資→換算。Remeasurement converts accounting-currency revenue/expense into the functional currency as if originally booked there, with the difference to net income (earnings impact); translation converts functional to reporting currency, difference to equity (CTA). Functional = home but operating overseas → remeasure; functional ≠ home, investing overseas → translate.

公司間 vs 公司內（Intercompany vs Intracompany）Intercompany vs Intracompany

兩者都由平衡機制自動補線，差別在「跨不跨主平衡段」。公司間（Intercompany）是跨不同主平衡段值（=不同公司別/法人），補的是公司間應收/應付。公司內（Intracompany）是同一主平衡段值內、但第二或第三平衡段不平衡時，用「額外平衡與清算選項」補公司內對消線。 Both auto-insert lines, differing on whether the primary balancing segment is crossed. 不一定，由交易類型決定。不需開票就直接轉 GL（一次提交可轉多個分類帳）；某些法域（尤其跨境）要求實體發票憑證，才走 AR→AP 開票路徑。No — the Transaction Type decides. No invoicing means direct transfer to GL (one submission can hit multiple ledgers); some jurisdictions (especially cross-border) require physical invoices, triggering the AR→AP path.

Smart View 可以直接在財報中心點開執行嗎？Can Smart View run directly from the FRC?▾

不行。Smart View 報表是 Excel 活頁簿，只能從財報中心下載成 Excel 後，用 Smart View 增益集登回 Financials 雲端刷新即時餘額。它上傳到 Catalog 只是為了集中分享與控管，不代表能在瀏覽器裡跑。No. A Smart View report is an Excel workbook; you must download it from the FRC, then the Smart View add-in logs back into Financials Cloud to refresh live balances. Uploading it to the catalog is only for centralized sharing/control, not in-browser execution.

餘額 Cube 需要我手動建立或維護嗎？Do I need to create or maintain the Balances Cube?▾

不用。它依圖表科目＋日曆組合自動建立，並由過帳、開期、換算三個標準 GL 程序自動更新。它取代了外部資料倉儲——你不必把資料匯出去外面算。No. It's auto-created per chart of accounts + calendar and auto-updated by three standard GL processes: posting, period opening, and translation. It replaces an external data warehouse — no need to export data elsewhere.

我有總帳會計角色，為什麼進 GL 還是看不到任何分類帳？I have the General Accountant role — why can't I see any ledger in GL?▾

因為角色只給功能，資料存取集才給分類帳。你需要第二步：到「管理使用者資料存取」把某個資料存取集綁到你的「使用者＋角色」組合。沒綁存取集，即使有角色也做不了事。Because the role grants function, but the Data Access Set grants ledgers. You need step two: in "Manage Data Access for Users," bind an access set to your user + role. Without it, the role alone lets you do nothing.

月底對帳報表顯示提供方有金額、接收方是空的，代表什麼？The recon report shows the provider has amounts but the receiver is empty — what does that mean?▾

代表接收方那端還沒完成它的部分——通常是還沒對它的公司間交易做 Create Accounting 或過帳。從報表鑽進差異、聯絡對方法人補做即可。注意公司間對帳報表用 BI 報表呈現，不能從「排程程序」頁提交。It means the receiver hasn't done its part — usually hasn't run Create Accounting or posted its intercompany transactions. Drill into the difference and ask that legal entity to complete it. Note the IC reconciliation report is BI-rendered and cannot be submitted from the Scheduled Processes page.

帳戶檢視器（Account Inspector）和財報有什麼不同？How is the Account Inspector different from a report?▾

帳戶檢視器是互動式即時查詢：拖拉 Cube 維度做樞紐、鑽到日記帳與子帳、匯出 Excel，數字隨條件即時刷新，適合臨時調查。正式財報（Web Studio）則是固定版面、可排程分批、爆發式 email 派送的董事會品質產物。The Account Inspector is interactive live inquiry: drag cube dimensions to pivot, drill to journals & subledger, export to Excel, amounts refreshing live — good for ad hoc investigation. A formal report (Web Studio) is fixed-layout, schedulable, batchable, burst-emailed boardroom output.
